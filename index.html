About Us

We Don't Just Capture
We Craft Feelings

"Emotions meet creativity — every wedding becomes a timeless story."

Welcome to Wedding Vibes Studio — where emotions meet creativity and every wedding becomes a timeless story. We are passionate wedding photographers & filmmakers dedicated to capturing the most beautiful moments of your special day with elegance, emotion, and cinematic style.

Based in Patna, Bihar, we specialize in Wedding Photography, Cinematic Wedding Films, Pre-Wedding Shoots, Candid Photography, and Traditional Coverage. Our goal is not just to take photos or videos, but to preserve the love, happiness, and memories that make your wedding unforgettable.

With creative vision, professional equipment, and a passionate team, we turn your wedding moments into cinematic masterpieces. ❤️🎬
